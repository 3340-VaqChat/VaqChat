About VaqChat UTRGV



The VaqChat program is a program centered around helping college students communicate effectively with other students while outside of the classroom. Through the use of a multi-tab GUI, databases, networking, and more, this VaqChat program offers the best in user-friendly capabilities.

With the help of this program, users are able to navigate through their own academic profiles, add their friends, upload their courses, chat, e-mail, and accomplish so much more.
